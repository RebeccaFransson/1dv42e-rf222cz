Instagram Statistics

Top three

This funktionallity gets your 20 most recent media on your Instagram then makes a calculation on witch of your pictures has the most likes.

Top three can change over time but old top threes will not save in the database.

Graphs

Your graphs will start out as nothing. but when you log in, once a day or so, the app will update the data and the graphs will start to show.

Not to worry!

If your become inactive the app will still update your data, but only once a week!
